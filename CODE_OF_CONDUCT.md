# Code of Conduct 🤝

## Our Pledge 🌟

We as members, contributors, and leaders of the **ICPHub DAO** community pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards ✨

### 🌱 **Examples of Positive Behavior**

- **Be Respectful**: Treat all community members with respect and kindness
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome newcomers and help them feel at home
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together constructively and share knowledge
- **Be Patient**: Everyone learns at their own pace
- **Be Professional**: Maintain professional communication in all interactions
- **Give Credit**: Acknowledge others' contributions and ideas
- **Accept Feedback**: Be open to constructive criticism and suggestions
- **Focus on the Community**: What is best for the overall community
- **Show Empathy**: Try to understand others' perspectives and situations

### 🚫 **Examples of Unacceptable Behavior**

- **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- **Discrimination**: Discriminatory language or behavior based on personal characteristics
- **Personal Attacks**: Insulting, derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- **Trolling**: Deliberate inflammatory, extraneous, or off-topic messages
- **Spam**: Unsolicited advertising, promotional content, or repetitive messaging
- **Doxxing**: Publishing private information without explicit permission
- **Sexual Content**: Inappropriate sexual language, imagery, or advances
- **Threats**: Threats of violence or harm, whether physical or emotional
- **Disruption**: Deliberately disrupting discussions or community activities
- **Plagiarism**: Taking credit for others' work without attribution

## Our Responsibilities 👮‍♀️

### **Community Leaders**

Project maintainers and community leaders are responsible for:

- **Clarifying Standards**: Explaining and enforcing community standards
- **Taking Action**: Responding to instances of unacceptable behavior
- **Fair Enforcement**: Applying consequences fairly and consistently
- **Creating Safe Spaces**: Ensuring the community remains welcoming and inclusive
- **Leading by Example**: Demonstrating positive behavior in all interactions

### **All Community Members**

Every community member is responsible for:

- **Following Guidelines**: Adhering to this Code of Conduct
- **Reporting Issues**: Speaking up when witnessing unacceptable behavior
- **Supporting Others**: Helping create a positive environment for everyone
- **Self-Reflection**: Considering how their actions affect others
- **Continuous Learning**: Growing and improving their community interactions

## Enforcement 🛡️

### **Reporting Process**

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it through one of these channels:

#### **Primary Contacts**
- **Email**: conduct@icphub.org
- **Direct Message**: Contact any core team member on Discord
- **Anonymous Form**: [Report Form](https://forms.gle/icphub-conduct) *(Coming Soon)*

#### **What to Include in Reports**
- **Detailed Description**: What happened and where
- **People Involved**: Who was involved (if comfortable sharing)
- **Evidence**: Screenshots, links, or other supporting materials
- **Impact**: How the behavior affected you or others
- **Previous Incidents**: Any related previous occurrences

### **Response Timeline**

- **Acknowledgment**: Within 24 hours
- **Initial Review**: Within 3 business days
- **Investigation**: Within 1-2 weeks (depending on complexity)
- **Resolution**: Communicated once investigation is complete

### **Investigation Process**

1. **Receipt**: We acknowledge receipt of the report
2. **Review**: Initial assessment of the report
3. **Investigation**: Gather information from all parties
4. **Decision**: Determine appropriate response
5. **Action**: Implement corrective measures
6. **Follow-up**: Check on the situation and prevent recurrence

---

## Enforcement Guidelines 📋

Community leaders will follow these guidelines when determining consequences:

### **1. Correction**
**Behavior**: Use of inappropriate language or unprofessional behavior
**Consequence**: 
- Private written warning
- Clarification of why behavior was inappropriate
- Public apology may be requested

### **2. Warning**
**Behavior**: Violation through a single incident or series of actions
**Consequence**:
- Warning with consequences for continued behavior
- No interaction with people involved for specified period
- Restrictions on community participation

### **3. Temporary Ban**
**Behavior**: Serious violation or continued inappropriate behavior
**Consequence**:
- Temporary ban from community interaction
- No public or private interaction with community members
- Duration determined by severity (typically 30-90 days)

### **4. Permanent Ban**
**Behavior**: Pattern of violations, severe single incident, or continued harassment
**Consequence**:
- Permanent ban from all community spaces
- No interaction with community members
- Legal action may be pursued if appropriate

---

## Appeals Process 📞

If you believe enforcement action was taken in error, you may appeal by:

1. **Submitting an Appeal**: Email appeals@icphub.org within 30 days
2. **Providing Evidence**: Include any relevant information or context
3. **Review Process**: Appeals are reviewed by different community leaders
4. **Decision**: Final decision communicated within 2 weeks

## Reporting Guidelines 📝

### **For Community Members**

#### **When to Report**
- You experience unacceptable behavior
- You witness unacceptable behavior
- You're unsure if something violates the Code of Conduct
- You want guidance on community standards

#### **How to Report Effectively**
- **Be Specific**: Provide clear details about what happened
- **Include Context**: Explain the situation and its impact
- **Provide Evidence**: Screenshots, links, or other documentation
- **Stay Factual**: Focus on observable behavior rather than interpretations
- **Protect Privacy**: Only share what's necessary for the investigation

### **For Report Recipients**

#### **Confidentiality**
- All reports are treated confidentially
- Information is only shared with those necessary for investigation
- Reporter identity is protected unless they consent to disclosure
- Anonymous reports are accepted and investigated

#### **No Retaliation**
- Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited
- Retaliation is considered a separate violation
- Additional protections may be implemented for reporters

## Community Guidelines 🌟

### **Communication Standards**

#### **Be Professional**
- Use clear, respectful language
- Stay on topic in discussions
- Provide constructive feedback
- Respect different opinions and perspectives

#### **Be Inclusive**
- Use inclusive language
- Avoid assumptions about others
- Welcome newcomers warmly
- Help create an accessible environment

#### **Be Collaborative**
- Share knowledge and resources
- Work together toward common goals
- Give and receive feedback gracefully
- Celebrate community achievements

### **Technical Discussions**

#### **Best Practices**
- Focus on technical merit
- Provide constructive criticism
- Share resources and documentation
- Help others learn and grow

#### **Avoid**
- Personal attacks over technical disagreements
- Gatekeeping or elitist behavior
- Dismissing others' contributions
- Taking credit for others' work
